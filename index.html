About Me

I do things for the web

I have been in love with programming ever since I wrote my first line of code to print 'hello world'.

I'm' a firm believer of Agile and have always worked under the Scrum Ecosystem. I'm an advocate for people-first, collaborative company culture, constant learning and open-source tools.
